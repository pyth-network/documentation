## Getting Started

Integrating Pyth price feeds into your application is quick and easy.

### Build 🧑‍💻

- Jump to our quick [tutorial](./create-your-first-pyth-app/) to get started with Pyth price feeds in your application.
- Consult our [guides](./use-real-time-data/) and selected the appropriate chain for your application.

**NOTE**: Pyth price feeds are permissionless and do not require a subscription or API key -- developers can complete the whole process in a few minutes.

### Learn 📚

- Check out [How Pyth Works](./how-pyth-works.mdx) to understand the architecture and design of Pyth price feeds.
- Interact with our on-chain methods at [API-reference](./api-reference.mdx)
